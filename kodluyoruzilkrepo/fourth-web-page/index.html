...Strawberry Magnolia...

You can prepare the Strawberry Magnoliat recipe, which will create a feast in your mouth with its cream and biscuits, in 30 minutes. If you want a light milky dessert in the summer months or if you want a fresh taste that will not be heavy in the winter months, you can choose this recipe. You can combine it with other fruits according to your wish and catch the taste you want.

Specification:Frontend Developer


People:4-6 People Preparation Time:30 Minutes Cooking time:10 Minutes Rest Time:4-6 hours

Necessary materials

  • 500 ml liter of milk
  • 2.5 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 1 whole and 1 half (yellow part only) egg
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 pack of liquid cream
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 packet of vanilla
  • 1 pack of cute baby biscuits
  • The fruit you want (Strawberry, banana, ...)

Making the Recipe

  1. Mix half a liter of milk and 2.5 tablespoons of starch.
  2. Pour 1 pack of cream.
  3. Add our egg, 3/4 cup of sugar and 1 full 1 yolk only.
  4. Put our pot on the stove and turn the bottom on medium heat.
  5. Keep stirring until it boils and turn the heat down after boiling.
  6. Continue to mix for 3 more minutes in a low way.
  7. Close the bottom and set it aside.
  8. Add 2 tablespoons of butter and 1 packet of vanilla into it.
  9. Take it aside to cool down and prepare the materials for the break.
  10. 1 pack of cute baby biscuits
  11. The fruit you want (Strawberry, banana, ...)

Tanning of Strawberry Magnolia

  • We add the sweet baby biscuits that we break into the medium-sized bowl of our choice.
  • We pour half of our cream on the biscuits and spread it.
  • We finely chop the fruit of our choice and lay it on top of the cream.
  • We add the remaining cream on the fruit and fix it.
  • We crumble and pour the remaining biscuits on the top layer.

Enjoy your meal
